# 1. Product Overview

## Introduction

Darik Pars Digital Platform is a modern financial website designed to introduce the company, showcase investment products, communicate financial insights, and create a trusted digital relationship with investors.

The platform is designed as the foundation of a future digital asset management ecosystem.

---

# 2. Product Vision

The product aims to transform a traditional corporate website into a professional investment platform that combines:

- Asset management expertise
- Investor education
- Financial transparency
- Digital experience
- Future AI capabilities

---

# 3. Business Goals

## Primary Goals

### Brand Growth

Build a premium digital identity for Darik Pars.

### Investor Acquisition

Convert visitors into qualified investor leads.

### Trust Building

Increase investor confidence through transparency.

### Digital Transformation

Prepare infrastructure for future financial technology products.

# 4. Target Users

---

# Persona 1: Individual Investor

## Description

A person looking for professional investment solutions.

## Goals

- Understand available products
- Compare funds
- Learn about investing
- Contact financial experts

## Pain Points

- Lack of financial knowledge
- Fear of wrong decisions
- Difficulty finding trusted institutions

---

# Persona 2: High Net Worth Investor

## Description

An investor with significant capital seeking professional management.

## Goals

- Portfolio management
- Customized strategies
- Confidential advisory

## Needs

- Professional communication
- Performance reporting
- Direct consultation

---

# Persona 3: Institutional Investor

## Description

Companies and organizations requiring investment solutions.

## Goals

- Risk management
- Investment planning
- Professional reporting

---

# Persona 4: Internal Admin Team

## Description

Darik Pars employees managing digital content.

## Needs

- Easy content management
- Fund management
- Team management
- Permission control
